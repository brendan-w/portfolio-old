I am currently working on my own electric guitar. Having been a guitar player for quite some time, I finally decided to combine my interests in guitar and woodworking. Styled after a Parker Fly, the body is made from alder, and carries Dimarzio pickups. While this project is far from finished, I am very excited about it, and am eagerly awaiting some off-time so I can complete it.
